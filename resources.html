As we know, the University of Ghana is the best university in Ghana. That being said and known, we lack no resoucres necessary for a good and perfect academic environment. Same goes for te Department of Computer Science.

The Department of Computer Science has a lot of equipment in order for every student to recieve the best education any institution can offer. COMPSA
The Computer Science Student Association of UG-LEGON is an association for all students in the department.The COMPSA is a separate entity that is run by department Staff and students.The association organizes annual congregations and elections which is approved by the HOD of the department.
