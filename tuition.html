TUITION

In University of Ghana, UG-LEGON, we have two types of students for those who are Ghanaians.We have the:

  • Regular Students
  • Full Fee paying Students

REGULAR

The Regular students are those who are able to meet the cutoff points or better still their results looks good. With this they do not pay a high amount of fees. In UG-LEGON, these are the fees for regular students as at 2023/2024 academic year.

NOTE:ALL in Ghana Currency

Freshman-100 Freshman-200 & 300 Continuing-200, 300 & 400
3,142.00 2,597.00 3,206.00

Full Fee paying Students

The Full Fee paying students on the other hands, are those who could not meet the cutoff point but yet still they have the potential to study the program based on their results. With this, they pay the full amout of fees.
